Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as transcription time?

Transcription time equals the length of the audio file. A 1-hour meeting uses 1 hour of your quota.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! Upgrade instantly, downgrade at your next billing cycle. Prorated billing ensures you only pay for what you use.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never train AI models on your private transcripts.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes! Annual Pro plans get 20% off. Contact us for custom Enterprise annual pricing.
